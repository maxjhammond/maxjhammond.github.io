Ethos

Minimal, Affordable, Beautiful

Professional websites today are problematic.

They're either over-designed or boring. Today the term "minimal" in the context of a website means an uninspired mess of pastel colours and rounded corners. Good looking websites are rare now. This is wrong.

My work is design-first, drawing inspiration from iconic graphic pieces such as album covers, magazines and advertisements.

Technology

What you need, nothing more

HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript

With the above I can create just about anything on a web page. For more dynamic content, we can introduce libraries like jQuery and Angular. For projects like blogs and online stores, we can introduce back-ends such as Wordpress, Magento and Shopify.
